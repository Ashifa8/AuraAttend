Our Face Recognition Attendance System is designed to revolutionize classroom attendance management for teachers. No more manual roll calls or proxy attendance—our smart system automatically marks attendance in Excel sheets by recognizing student faces, ensuring accuracy and saving valuable time.

For students, we provide a dedicated portal where they can access their attendance records and share feedback with teachers, fostering a transparent and efficient attendance process. This system offers real-time attendance tracking, allowing both students and teachers to manage and monitor their attendance records effectively. It’s simple to use and saves time, making the learning environment more efficient and focused.
